Campus Policy

Rules & regulations

All students are expected to adhere to the rules and regulations of the college, on and off the academic calendar.

Folio 01 — Dress Code & Conduct

General conduct

Students, both boys and girls, should attend college properly dressed — boys in pants and shirts, girls in a saree or churidhar with dupatta. T-shirts, jeans and other casuals are strictly prohibited inside the campus, and uniforms are required for all practical classes.

  • Be regular and punctual to both theory and practical classes.
  • Be seated at least 5 minutes before the bell.
  • Stand when a staff member enters the class, as a mark of respect.
  • Maintain proper decorum while classes are in progress.
  • Attend all tests and submit assignments on time — those who abstain from tests will be penalised.
Folio 02 — Discipline

Discipline policy

Smoking, consumption of alcohol, gambling and other unlawful activities are totally prohibited inside the campus; any violation can lead to dismissal from the college. A student convicted of, or arrested in connection with, any offence will not be permitted to join or resume studies.

A disciplinary committee — convened by the Vice-Principal, with heads of departments as members — investigates any charges against students. The committee's recommendation is accepted in full, with action taken by the Principal accordingly.

Folio 03 — Anti-Ragging

Ragging policy

Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited by the Government of India and the Government of Tamil Nadu. Under existing government orders, a student reported to be involved in ragging will be expelled from the college without further enquiry. The college follows government directives in full for any such incident.
