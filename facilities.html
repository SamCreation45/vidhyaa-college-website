College Facilities Register

Everything a student needs, built block by block in Konganapuram.

From well-stocked laboratories to a fleet of college buses, this is the working record of what supports every student at Vidhyaa Arts & Science College — affiliated to Periyar University, Salem.

30 Buses across six routes

Facilities on record

7 entries
Folio 01 — Infrastructure

Buildings

The college is being constructed in a well-designed manner, with dedicated blocks for each department and good aeration throughout. Special coaching classes and beauty care classes run alongside the regular academic schedule.

Present infrastructure in the laboratories is sufficient for high-tech research, and a new round block has been designed specifically to house the library and computer laboratory.

Folio 02 — Athletics

Sports

Students are encouraged to take part in National level, University level, Inter-collegiate and open tournaments wherever they are held. Vidhyaa students have won prizes across Table Tennis, Badminton, Basketball, Cricket and other sporting events.

Folio 03 — Financial Aid

Scholarships

Backward Class scholarships, Adi Dravidar Welfare scholarships, National Loan scholarships, State Loan scholarships and select merit scholarships are available to eligible students. Applicants from backward communities, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes may apply through the college for sanction.

College administration follows up directly with officials on behalf of applicants, and a number of students additionally receive scholarships from private trusts and agencies with the college's assistance.

Folio 04 — Policy

Attendance

Regular attendance is required; students arriving late are not permitted into class. One period of absence in a session (forenoon or afternoon) is reckoned as half a day for attendance purposes, and a minimum of 75% attendance is required in both theory and practical classes to sit university examinations.

Attendance maintained by each chief faculty advisor is the valid record. Medical certificates are considered only in genuine cases for leave, and are not factored into the attendance calculation itself.

Folio 05 — Reading Room

Library

A spacious, well-furnished reading hall sits at the centre of the college. Students may borrow books for reference — the borrowing limit is announced at the start of each academic year, and books may be retained for ten days before a return is due.

The library subscribes to a wide range of international and national periodicals, and holds a strong collection of CDs and newspapers. Overdue books are fined per day of delay, and students are expected to handle borrowed books with care — marking, underlining or otherwise damaging library books is prohibited and heavily fined. Every visitor signs the register on entry, and rule violations can lead to cancellation of a borrower's ticket.

Folio 06 — Laboratories

Lab Facilities

Computer Science, Textile, and Physics each run fully stacked, well-equipped laboratories built to Periyar University's syllabus requirements.

Computer Science

  • Computer & microprocessor lab, Operating Systems lab, Case Tools lab
  • Networking lab and Hardware lab
  • System Software & Database lab, Compiler Design lab, Multimedia lab
  • Internet lab with 24-hour access, plus campus-wide Wi-Fi for project and seminar work
  • After-hours lab access permitted under faculty guidance; lab uniform required on lab days

Physics

  • Advanced lab facilities with fully working equipment
  • Students carry out practicals individually under professors' guidance

Textile & Fashion Designing

Established in 2011 to give students the technical grounding for careers across textile processing and apparel industries.

  • Modernised sewing lab and CAD lab
  • Textile testing and processing lab
  • Fashion sketching lab, plus a beauty & body care lab
  • Guest lecture programmes and industrial visits
  • Seminars, fashion shows and workshops
Folio 07 — Logistics

Transport

A full-fledged transport department runs a fleet of buses for students and staff, ensuring safe, hassle-free travel to and from campus. The college currently operates 30 buses, with routes covering Konganapuram, Salem, Erode, Namakkal, Omalur and Edappadi — expanding as student intake grows.
